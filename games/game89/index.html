How to Play Balloons Shooter

Welcome to Balloons Shooter, a bright and fast reflex training application built on clean arc-angle physics loops. The canvas engine generates floating target spheres that drift up from the lower screen edge at random speeds. Players use mouse or touch interactions to aim a targeting arrow, projecting a line vector that tracks the trajectory of your shots. Clicking triggers a raycast check against the balloon collision spheres to instantly pop targets and add to your score. The fast event loops ensure zero mouse lag, offering a snappy experience that keeps players highly engaged.
